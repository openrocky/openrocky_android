OpenRocky Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

OpenRocky is an open-source AI agent application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. OpenRocky operates entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers because we don't have any servers.

Third-Party AI Providers

OpenRocky connects to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) using API keys that you provide. When you send messages:

  • Your messages are sent directly to the AI provider you configured
  • The AI provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data
  • We do not intercept, store, or relay your conversations through any intermediary

On-Device Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • API Keys: Stored in Android EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • Conversations: Stored as local files in the app's private directory
  • Memory: Key-value pairs stored locally
  • Settings: Provider configurations, character settings, skill definitions
  • Logs: Debug logs stored locally, auto-deleted after 7 days

All this data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

OpenRocky requests the following permissions for its tool capabilities:

  • Internet: To communicate with AI providers
  • Location: For weather and nearby search tools (optional)
  • Contacts: For contact search tool (optional)
  • Calendar: For calendar and reminder tools (optional)
  • Microphone: For voice input (optional)
  • Camera: For camera capture tool (optional)
  • Notifications: For scheduled notification tool (optional)

All permissions are optional. The app functions without them — individual tools will simply indicate that permission is needed.

Children's Privacy

OpenRocky is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Open Source

OpenRocky is open source. You can review the complete source code at github.com/openrocky.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
